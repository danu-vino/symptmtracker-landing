How It Works

One minute. Every day.

Open the app, tap how you feel, select any symptoms — done. The daily check-in is designed to be effortless so you'll actually stick with it. No typing required.

Mood in a tap

Rate your day from Very Good to Very Bad with expressive mood faces.

Symptoms at a glance

Tap to select from common symptoms like fatigue, headache, anxiety, or joint pain.

Gentle reminders

Set a daily reminder at your preferred time so you never miss a check-in.

Patterns & Trends

See what your body is telling you.

Over time, your check-ins reveal patterns you might not notice on your own. Symaura connects the dots — showing you which symptoms appear together, how your mood shifts, and what your body's been through.

Health overview

Your total entries, average mood, and mood stability at a glance.

Pattern detection

Automatically spots correlations like "muscle pain and dizziness often occur together."

Most common symptoms

Ranked list of what comes up most, so you know what to discuss with your doctor.
